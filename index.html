Transforming Enclosed Infrastructure

Through state-of-the-art inspection, cleaning, and digitization, we are making operations and maintenance of water pipelines, sewers, stormwater drains, manholes, and septic tanks smarter and safer. Since 2018, we’ve been revolutionizing underground infrastructure for a cleaner, more efficient Water and Sanitation landscape.

We are solving problems in
Sewer Lines
Wastewater treatment is a critical part of the WASH landscape. These networks cope with challenges like aging, blockages, and root intrusions.
Stormwater Drain
Stormwater drains help prevent urban flooding. They face issues due to blockages from debris and silt.
Manual Scavenging
Despite legal checks, manual scavenging persists. We intend to solve this through robotics built with purpose.
Water Pipelines
Every year, 126 billion cubic meters of water is lost. Pipeline inspections identify issues early.
Non-revenue Water
NRW causes $39 billion loss annually. Our aim is to use robotics and digitization to address this.
